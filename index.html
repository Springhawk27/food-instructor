Light Thai Soup

  1. Mix ginger & garlic paste with the chicken. Fry them in a pan with 1 tsp of vegetable oil for 5 mins. Then put them in a separate bowl.
  2. Take and pour 5 cups of cold water in a saucepan. Mix the corn flour with water. Then pour all the ingredients one by one. Once done with mixing put the pan on the cooker. Keep it on high flame for 5 minutes and keep stir in.
  3. When the soup will start boiling turn the flame on low. Once it thicken turn of the gas. Serve in a soup bowl.
  4. This soup can be served with baguette or normal bread.
